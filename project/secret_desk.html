Trapdoor System (in Progress)
A secret trapdoor system for swapping items

Project Overview

The system features a robotic arm designed to perform a high-precision magic trick. It utilizes a hidden trapdoor mechanism (or a secret compartment) to execute a seamless, undetectable switch. After the magic effect is completed, the robotic arm moves the gimmicked prop into the secret hatch and instantly swaps it for an identical regular object. This allows the item to be handed over for a full audience inspection, ensuring the secret mechanism remains completely hidden.
